The ceremony & celebration

The Lodge at Ashford Castle

Set within the ancient estate on the shores of Lough Corrib, The Lodge is where we'll gather to get married. Intimate and unhurried, it sits at the edge of the woodland with views across the river and grounds — the right place for something as personal as this.

The estate

Ashford Castle

The Lodge and the Castle share the same grounds — guests are free to roam between the two. Stone corridors, candlelit dining rooms, and a mile of private lakeshore are all yours to enjoy across the weekend. A number of rooms within the Castle have been reserved; booking details will follow with your invitation.

Getting There

Travel & Accommodations

Ashford Castle sits on the shores of Lough Corrib in the heart of County Mayo — one of Ireland's most breathtaking corners. We have gathered everything you need to make your journey as smooth as possible.

Getting Here

Flying In

The nearest airport is Ireland West Airport Knock (NOC), approximately 45 minutes from Ashford Castle. Dublin Airport (DUB) is roughly 2.5 hours by car. We recommend hiring a driver or renting a car to enjoy the beautiful countryside drive.

Where to Stay

Staying at Ashford Castle

We have reserved a room block at The Lodge at Ashford Castle. Please mention "Laura & Andrew Wedding" when calling +353 94 954 0003 or emailing [email protected]. Block rates available until May 1, 2027.

Nearby Options

Local Accommodations

For those seeking alternatives nearby, we recommend Cong Village (a short walk from the castle), Ballinrobe, or Castlebar. A shuttle service will run between the castle and Cong village throughout the weekend.
